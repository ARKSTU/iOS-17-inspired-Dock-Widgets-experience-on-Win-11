# Privacy Policy for iOS 17 Inspired Launcher – Ray Studio Edition

**Effective Date:** December 7, 2024  
**Last Updated:** December 7, 2024

## Overview

iOS 17 Inspired Launcher – Ray Studio Edition ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Data Collection

### Required Permissions

The App requires certain Android permissions to function as a launcher:

- **QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES**: To display and manage installed applications
- **PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS**: To provide smart app suggestions based on usage patterns (opt-in)
- **BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE**: To display notifications in the Notification Center (opt-in)
- **VIBRATE**: To provide haptic feedback
- **SET_WALLPAPER**: To manage wallpapers
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**: To start launcher services on device boot

### Data We Collect

1. **App Usage Data** (Local Only - Opt-in)
   - Frequency of app launches
   - App usage patterns for smart suggestions
   - This data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted

2. **Preferences and Settings** (Local Only)
   - Your launcher configuration (grid size, themes, etc.)
   - Widget arrangements
   - Stored locally on your device

3. **Notification Data** (Local Only - Opt-in)
   - Notification content for Notification Center display
   - Stored temporarily and cleared when dismissed
   - Never transmitted from your device

## Data We DO NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your contacts, messages, or call logs
- We do not transmit any data to external servers
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not share data with third parties

## Data Usage

All data collected by the App:
- Is stored locally on your device
- Is used solely to provide launcher functionality
- Is never transmitted to external servers
- Is deleted when you uninstall the App

## Telemetry (Opt-In)

If you choose to enable telemetry:
- Basic crash reports may be sent to help improve the App
- Reports are anonymized and contain no personal information
- You can disable telemetry at any time in Settings
- **Telemetry is disabled by default**

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for general audiences and does not target children.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect data stored locally on your device. However, no method of storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (by uninstalling the App or clearing app data)
- Opt out of optional features (usage stats, notification access, telemetry)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by updating the "Last Updated" date.
